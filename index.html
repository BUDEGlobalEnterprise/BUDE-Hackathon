About BUDE Global Hackathons

Welcome to the BUDE Global Hackathon platform! We host regular hackathons to encourage contributions to our open source projects and build a stronger community.

Each hackathon features:

  • Real-time leaderboards tracking contributions
  • Multiple repositories to contribute to
  • Prizes and recognition for top contributors
  • A welcoming community of developers
